What we build?

New wells, epair old wells, build rain collection tanks, preserve springs, and place sand dams as needed. We provide our in-country specialists, as well as the communities themselves, the authority to decide what makes sense. Over the last 11 years, we've discovered that each community we serve is unique. Many factors influence the best sustainable means of providing access to clean and safe water, ranging from basic geology to climate and culture. Your contributions enable effective community mobilization, thorough project development, and long-term monitoring and assessment.

What's the cost ?

We're devoted to investing in the most effective, efficient, and long-term solutions possible. Catching rain, protecting a spring, or fixing a broken pump may be less expensive and more valuable than drilling a new well. We've also discovered that projects that appear to be low-cost frequently overlook the most critical parts of community building and long-term impact. We make certain that our teams take a holistic approach and are accountable over time. Every donation is used toward a specific project that you will see. We'd love to chat to you if you're interested in supporting a complete project.
